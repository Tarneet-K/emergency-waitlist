Hospital Triage App

A simple tool to help emergency staff organize patients by urgency and help patients understand their expected wait time.

Choose how you want to continue

Select your role below. You can switch between Patient and Triage Staff at any time.

I am a Patient

Answer a few quick questions about your injury and pain level so the triage staff can place you in the queue and estimate your wait time.

  • Select injury type and pain level
  • Submit a triage entry in under a minute
  • Information is visible only to hospital staff

I am Triage Staff (Admin)

View the full patient queue, assign room and priority levels, and keep the waiting room informed as patients are treated.

  • See all patients ordered by priority & arrival
  • Update triage level and assign rooms
  • Remove patients once treatment is complete
